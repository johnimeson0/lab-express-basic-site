About Kid Cudi

Scott Mescudi, better known by his stage name Kid Cudi, is an American singer, songwriter, rapper, record producer, and actor from Cleveland, Ohio. He is widely recognized for his influence on modern hip hop and alternative music. His most popular tracks being "Day n' Nite", "Pursuit of Happiness", "Ghost Town" with Kanye West, "Mr. Rager", and many more. 5 of Cudi's records have hit top 10 in billboard, and as of June of 2022 Cudi had sold over 22 million records in the U.S.
